# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Recruiters, hiring managers, and technical reviewers evaluating Jinni Li's readiness for graduate or junior systems, data infrastructure, and AI infrastructure roles. They need a fast, credible read of her technical direction, project depth, experience, and communication style.

## Product Purpose

This portfolio presents Jinni as an editorial, systems-oriented builder whose work connects data, infrastructure, AI, and product-minded engineering. Success means a visitor can quickly understand her trajectory, trust the accuracy of her project claims, and find relevant project, experience, GitHub, LinkedIn, and email paths without friction.

## Brand Personality

Editorial, technical, warm, systems-oriented, product-minded, and slightly experimental while remaining recruiter-readable.

## Anti-references

Do not become a generic SaaS landing page, a design-studio-only portfolio, a dense CV page, or a chaotic abstract poster. Avoid heavy motion, vague inflated claims, generic card grids, excessive decoration, and anything that reduces recruiter readability.

## Design Principles

- Preserve the data-garden / systems-map identity while keeping content legible and intentional.
- Let technical proof lead: projects, architecture, and experience should feel concrete and recruiter-safe.
- Use warmth through texture, typography, and restrained palette rather than visual clutter.
- Keep experimental moments focused, especially in the hero and technical direction section.
- Make navigation and calls to action obvious without flattening the editorial tone.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for WCAG AA readability, reduced-motion support where relevant, no heavy motion, and mobile layouts with no horizontal overflow.
